What is Hackathon '21?

It is a 2 day coding event where participants solve the given problem statement with their team to compete with others. What if the most creative minds siphoned off into solving the most impactful problems of today? Here comes our traditional hackathon with a situational update (Online Mode) which is an invention marathon, a collaborative software or hardware development competition, where teams of hackers work for multiple days to create a prototype or efficient and representable product. Winning teams are awarded with cash prizes and exciting goodies from our sponsors.
